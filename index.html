Simulated Tickets

Ticket 001 – Login Issue

Issue: Customer cannot access web portal after multiple login attempts.

Steps Taken:

  • Verified customer identity and account status.
  • Checked for active service outages affecting login.
  • Guided customer through password reset process.
  • Ensured account was unlocked and credentials updated.

Tools/Skills Applied: Zendesk, Email support, Troubleshooting, Communication, Problem-solving

Resolution: Customer successfully logged in.

Sample Reply: "Hello [Customer Name], your account has been successfully reset. Please try logging in and let us know if any issues remain."

Ticket 002 – Report Generation Error

Issue: Monthly report fails to generate for finance department.

Steps Taken:

  • Replicated issue in test environment.
  • Checked user permissions and report configuration.
  • Cleared cache and retried report generation.
  • Escalated to Tier 2 for deeper technical review.

Tools/Skills Applied: Jira, Troubleshooting, Communication, Workflow Coordination

Resolution: Issue resolved after Tier 2 intervention, report generated successfully.

Sample Reply: "Hello [Customer Name], the report generation issue has been resolved. Please check your report and confirm everything looks correct."

Ticket 003 – Password Reset

Issue: Customer forgot password and is locked out of account.

Steps Taken:

  • Verified customer identity using security questions.
  • Reset password through ticketing system.
  • Confirmed customer could log in successfully.
  • Provided tips for future password management.

Tools/Skills Applied: Zendesk, Email support, Customer Communication, Problem-solving

Resolution: Customer access restored.

Sample Reply: "Hi [Customer Name], your password has been reset, and you should now have access. Let us know if any issues persist."

Ticket 004 – Application Bug

Issue: Customer reports unexpected error when submitting form.

Steps Taken:

  • Gathered screenshots and error logs from customer.
  • Tested form submission in staging environment.
  • Identified misconfiguration causing error.
  • Escalated to the developer team with detailed notes.

Tools/Skills Applied: Jira, Bug Reporting, Technical Troubleshooting, Communication

Resolution: Bug fixed by the developer team, customer confirmed submission successful.

Sample Reply: "Hello [Customer Name], the issue with the form has been resolved. Please try submitting again and let us know if it works."
